Here's a timeline of Pramit Amatya:

  • 2002:This is the year Pramit was born.
  • 2009:This was the year Pramit had bike accident.
  • 2013:This is the year Pramit participated in his first national level elite chess tournament
  • 2014:This is the year Pramit became U-12 national chess champion.
  • 2018:This is the year Pramit went to India, Assam for the first time to play a tournament.
  • 2019:This is the year Pramit was improving his game dramatically and went for a chess tour of 3 tournament in India(Rajesthan,Gujarat,Mumbai). And this is the year Pramit stopped playing chess.
  • 2020: Corona time
  • 2021:Pramit Joined IT in NCCS college
  • 2022-2023:This is the year Pramit felt accepted and felt one of them. Lived youth life finally. Pramit was so so happy. But he realised the time has come. So he started the game
  • 2024:He will achieve what he desires.
